Welcome to My World!

I'm Dan Dougherty, a UX Designer with a strong background in customer service. Trained at CareerFoundry, I specialize in user research, usability testing, wireframing, prototyping, and interaction design.

I use design thinking to solve complex problems and create seamless user journeys. My strengths include high-fidelity prototypes, user interviews, and heuristic evaluations, with a portfolio focused on accessibility and engagement.

I'm seeking a junior UX designer role to apply my skills in user-centered design, information architecture, and visual design within a dynamic team.

Outside of design, I have a passion for working on cars, where my problem-solving abilities and attention to detail are as evident as they are in my design work.
