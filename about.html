Welcome to My World!

I'm Dan Dougherty, a UX Designer with a strong background in customer service. My training at CareerFoundry honed my skills in user research, usability testing, wireframing, prototyping, and interaction design.

I apply design thinking to solve complex problems, ensuring seamless user journeys. I excel in creating high-fidelity prototypes, conducting user interviews, and performing heuristic evaluations. My portfolio features projects optimizing user interfaces for accessibility and engagement.

I'm seeking a junior UX designer role to leverage my skills in user-centered design, information architecture, and visual design, aiming to contribute to a dynamic team.

When not designing, I enjoy working on cars, where my problem-solving skills and attention to detail shine, much like in my design work.

Unique Attributes:

Problem Solving

With extensive experience in the fast-paced customer service industry, particularly in restaurants, I have honed a robust ability to quickly identify and resolve issues, ensuring customer satisfaction and operational efficiency. This background has equipped me with a unique perspective in UX design, where anticipating user needs and solving usability challenges are crucial.

Collaboration

My enthusiasm for teamwork is rooted in my belief that diverse ideas lead to innovative solutions. I thrive in collaborative environments and often take the lead in projects, ensuring that multiple perspectives are considered and that the iterative design process is thorough and effective.

Continuous Learning

As a lifelong learner, I immerse myself in the ever-evolving field of UX/UI design by regularly engaging with industry articles and blogs. This commitment keeps me updated on the latest trends and technologies, enabling me to apply contemporary design practices to my projects.
