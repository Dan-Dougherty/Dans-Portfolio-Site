Welcome to My World!

I'm Dan Dougherty. As a UX Designer with a rich background in customer service, I've translated my passion for serving others into creating user-friendly digital solutions. My training at CareerFoundry has equipped me with a skill set that includes user research, usability testing, and elegant wireframing, all exemplified in my project, PlutoPay, designed to streamline online financial transactions.

I'm on the lookout for a junior UX designer role where I can apply my knack for problem-solving and user-centered design. When I'm not immersed in UX, you'll find me under the hood of a car, fine-tuning my ability to innovate and tackle complex problems, much like in my design work.

Unique Attributes:

With extensive experience in the fast-paced customer service industry, particularly in restaurants, I have honed a robust ability to quickly identify and resolve issues, ensuring customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.
